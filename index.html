About

Profile

Physician–epidemiologist and global health leader with two decades of experience advancing vaccine development, evaluation, and implementation across Africa, Asia, and Europe. Expertise spans clinical trials, real-world effectiveness, disease-burden estimation, and policy translation, with a strong record of guiding multidisciplinary teams and multi-country partnerships from study design to public-health impact. Combines rigorous quantitative epidemiology with strategic leadership to accelerate evidence generation, inform global immunization policy, and strengthen research capacity in resource-limited settings.
